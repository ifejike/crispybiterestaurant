About Us


Crispy Bite has a rich history that spans decades, and like the iconic Mick Jagger, we're still going strong. Our legacy is deeply rooted in the fabric of rock and roll, thanks to a group of visionary individuals who dreamed of creating a welcoming gathering place for all. Dubbed the hub of music and tasty meals, Crispy Bite has consistently united people from all walks of life through our passion for great food, lively ambiance, and the universal language of music.


With over 180 locations globally, Crispy Bite is the ultimate destination for events of all sizes. Whether you're planning an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, our team is dedicated to bringing your vision to life. From milestone events like bar mitzvahs and anniversaries to corporate team-building and pre-wedding parties, we collaborate with you to create a personalized and unforgettable experience that's truly rockstar-worthy.
